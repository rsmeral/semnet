Semantic network of artworks

Data

The collected data in RDF format and the SeRQL queries that were used to retrieve the data from the store. These do not include inferred statements (WordNet class hierarchy).

Actors and directors from CSFD.cz. (SERQL)

Films from CSFD.cz. (SERQL)

lit-works.rdf.zip (4 MB)

Literary works from DatabazeKnih.cz. (SERQL)
